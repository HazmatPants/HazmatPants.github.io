GI-55 AMR

The GI-55 Anti-Materiel Rifle is a precision-engineered, heavy-caliber anti-materiel rifle designed by Grigga Industries for long-range engagements against fortified targets, light vehicles, and hardened equipment. Firing the potent .55 GI Caseless round, this weapon is a powerhouse in both urban and open-field combat, tailored for highly trained military snipers and specialized anti-materiel units.
